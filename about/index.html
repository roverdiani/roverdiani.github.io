ABOUT

Hey there!

Hey, I’m Rodrigo Verdiani, and this is my blog! :D

I’m a twenty-something Brazilian tech enthusiast, currently working as a frontend developer, specializing in React.

I was born in the countryside of São Paulo, Brazil, about 50 km away from the capital, and grew up mostly with my grandparents. My grandpa was a science teacher and later a programmer for the city’s water department. He’s the one who got me into computers and electronics!

My main tech interests are (in no particular order):

  • Graphics Programming
  • Game Engine Programming
  • AI
  • Quantum Computing
  • Retro tech

Besides that, some of my hobbies are:

  • Role Playing Games
  • Electronics (mainly audio stuff and old digital tech, like Z80s, 6502, etc)
  • Music
  • Photography

The idea behind this website is pretty simple: to share things I learn, love, and think might be useful to others. It also serves as a portfolio or a journal of all the stuff I’ve tinkered with.

In an older version of this blog, I mentioned that I couldn’t pick a favorite programming language. These days, though, I mostly write my personal code in C. I really enjoy how it lets me focus more on logic and structure, thanks to its simplicity.

I hope you find my content interesting! If you have any suggestions, spot something that’s off, or run into any issues, feel free to let me know. Since English isn’t my first language, I’d really appreciate some feedback. :P

Thanks for reading this far! See ya!
